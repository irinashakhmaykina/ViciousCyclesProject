Biking is for Everyone!

Vicious Cycles is dedicated to providing people with affordable bikes and supplies. Our mission is to spread the love that we have for biking and make sure that it is a hobby everyone has the ability to explore. We believe that biking doesn't have to necessarily be a sport but instead can be something you simply enjoy doing. Stress free. Our bikes are made for leisure and incredibly affordable to anyone wanting to start. We are located in the heart of Raleigh and are open to beginners. We will help you with anything you might need because our community is a no-stress one whether you're just now getting into biking, or you've been doing it for years.
