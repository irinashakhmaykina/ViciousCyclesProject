About Us

We are a small group of bike lovers who want to spread our love of biking to the world. Our founders were born in a small town in North Carolina where one of their favorite activities was biking. Now they wish to share their hobby with the world. We love to inspire young and old people and show them the joys that biking has. We and our loving staff are always there to help you pick out your first bicycle!

Our Philosophy

We believe that everyone deserves the chance to start a hobby such as biking in a non expensive manner. That is why we offer some of the cheapest but high-quality bicycles on the market. No matter what, everyone deserves a chance to bike and travel from one place to another in an affordable manner. We will help you pick out the bike you want as well as anything else you might need. This includes changinf the wheels and even customizing your bicycle. We will teach you how to become a pro as soon as you walk into our shop because everyone deserves that opportunity.
